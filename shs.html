Student Enrollment by Track in 2nd Semester

Track Description
Science Technology Engineering and Mathematics STEM encompasses science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, fostering hands-on learning and problem-solving skills for future careers in fields like robotics, computer science, and biotechnology.
Accountancy Business Management Accountancy Business Management integrates accounting principles with essential business skills, preparing graduates for diverse roles in finance, corporate management, and strategic decision-making.
Humanities and Social Science Humanities and Social Sciences study human society, culture, and behavior through disciplines like literature, history, psychology, and economics, providing insights into our past and present and fostering understanding and informed citizenship.
Sheilded Metal Arc Weilding Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW), or stick welding, joins metals by creating an electric arc between a flux-coated electrode and the workpiece. It's versatile, portable, and commonly used for welding thick materials in construction, pipelines, and fabrication.
Electrical Installation Maintenance Electrical Installation Maintenance involves the inspection, repair, and upkeep of electrical systems and components to ensure their proper functioning and safety. It includes tasks such as installing wiring, troubleshooting electrical faults, replacing faulty components, and performing routine maintenance checks.
Automotive Servicing Automotive servicing involves the maintenance, repair, and inspection of vehicles to ensure their optimal performance, safety, and longevity. This includes tasks such as oil changes, tire rotations, brake repairs, engine diagnostics, and general vehicle inspections. Automotive servicing technicians use specialized tools and equipment to diagnose issues and perform necessary repairs, helping to keep vehicles running smoothly and efficiently.
Computer System Servicing Computer System Servicing encompasses the maintenance, repair, and optimization of computer systems to ensure their efficient operation and longevity. This involves tasks such as hardware installation, software updates, virus removal, troubleshooting, and system diagnostics. Computer technicians use specialized tools and software to diagnose issues and perform necessary repairs, ensuring that computers operate smoothly and securely.
